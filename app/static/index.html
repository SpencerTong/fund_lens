How it works

1

Enter your numbers

Fill in the form fields — revenue, margins, runway, headcount. Nothing is required beyond company name and revenue; the more you add, the richer the analysis.

2

Live market data + your numbers → Claude

fund-lens fetches current interest rates, VIX, S&P 500 performance, and public SaaS valuation multiples from Yahoo Finance, then combines them with your metrics into a structured prompt. Claude receives both your company data and today's market context.

3

You get structured, actionable output

The response is parsed into clearly labelled sections — narrative analysis, key insights, risk flags, and recommendations — so it's ready to act on, not just read.

4

Ask follow-ups (Ask tab only)

The Ask tab keeps a running conversation history. Each follow-up is sent with the full prior context, so Claude remembers what was already discussed — just like talking to a human advisor.

Tips for best results

Analyze

Use Focus Areas to direct the AI — checking "Fundraising Readiness" gives very different output than "Burn Efficiency".

Ask CFO

Fill in the Context fields (ARR, stage, industry) before asking. Grounded context produces specific answers, not generic advice.

Compare

You don't need identical fields for both companies. Claude handles missing data gracefully and notes where gaps limit the comparison.

General

Use the Fill Example button on any tab to see a realistic sample and understand what the output looks like before entering your own data.
